Documents Needed

  • featured_play_list Bece Result

    The Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) result is mandatory for students transitioning from Junior Secondary School (JSS) to Senior Secondary School (SSS). It shows the student's performance in the final exams of JSS and determines eligibility for SS1. You'll need to present your BECE certificate or result slip during the admission process.

  • import_contacts Birth Certificate

    This document is used to verify the student's age and identity, ensuring that the student meets the age requirements for admission into Our School. Contacting the vital records office in the jurisdiction where the birth occurred and submitting an application along with any required documentation.

  • chrome_reader_mode Application letter

    Application for Admission to Akintan Senior Grammar School.

  • person Passport photo

    Passport-sized photographs are used for identification purposes on admission forms, school ID cards, and other official records. We need 2 passport of the student and one passport of the parent/guardian.

  • receipt Tax payment receipt

    A tax payment receipt is required as proof of the parent/guardian's tax compliance, especially beacuse Akintan' free education is partially funded by state taxes.Your tax payment should not exceed 6 months.



Admission Process:

  1. Primary Education Completion: To be eligible for Akintan Grammar School, students must complete six years of primary education and the three-year junior secondary education.
  2. Application and Registration: Parents or guardians need to register their ward at the Education district 4 Unit(8 McEwen St, Sabo yaba, Lagos 101245, Lagos) for the transition into Senior Secondary School (SS1).
  3. Placement and Entrance Examinations: Candidates seeking admission are often required to pass an entrance examination after approval from district 4 , particularly if they are transitioning from a different junior secondary school.
  4. Documentation: Parents must provide relevant documents, including the birth certificate, last school report, primary school leaving certificate, and others as specified by the school, which will be verified by the school authorities.
  5. Acceptance and Enrollment: Upon acceptance of the offer, parents complete the enrollment process by paying any stipulated fees and ensuring compliance with the school's rules and regulations.
