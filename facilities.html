Chemistry Laboratory

The chemistry lab is equipped with essential tools and reagents to support students in conducting practical experiments, such as titration, chemical reactions, and compound analysis. It provides a safe environment with proper ventilation, safety gear, and fume cupboards to promote hands-on learning of theoretical concepts.

Biology Laboratory

The biology lab offers a variety of specimens, microscopes, and models to aid students in studying life sciences. It facilitates experiments related to cell biology, genetics, ecology, and anatomy, giving learners practical insights into the structure and functions of living organisms.

ICT Laboratory

Experience dinnings made just for you
The ICT lab is equipped with computers, high-speed internet, and educational software to enhance digital literacy and technical skills. Students engage in activities ranging from coding and data analysis to research and multimedia production, preparing them for the digital world.
