Welcome to Akintan Senior Grammar School – A center of academic excellence and holistic development in the heart of Surulere, Lagos. Founded on the core principles of discipline, integrity, and innovation, Akintan has consistently nurtured generations of students to become well-rounded, responsible citizens and future leaders.

Principal’s Welcome Note

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our school’s website. At Akintan, we are committed to fostering a nurturing environment where every student is encouraged to achieve academic excellence and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty, comprehensive curriculum, and vibrant extracurricular programs are designed to develop well-rounded individuals prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow. We believe in empowering our students to become future leaders, guided by the values of discipline, integrity, and service to the community. Thank you for being part of our journey, and we look forward to partnering with you in shaping the next generation of achievers.
